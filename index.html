Castle Of Chapultepec

chapultepec castle is a historic castle located on chapultepec hill in mexico city. built on 1785, it is the only royal castle in north america that was used as a residence for emperors. it played an important role in the battle of chapultepec during the mexican-american war in 1847 and later became the home of emperor maximilian I. today, it is the national museum of history, where visitors can learn about mexico history and enjoy beautiful view of the city.
